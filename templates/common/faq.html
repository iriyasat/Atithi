Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

For Guests

To book a property, simply browse our listings, select your desired dates, and click the "Book Now" button. You'll need to create an account if you haven't already, and then follow the booking process which includes payment.

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, bKash, and other popular mobile banking services in Bangladesh.

Our cancellation policy varies by property. You can find the specific cancellation policy for each property on its listing page. Generally, cancellations made 48 hours before check-in are eligible for a full refund.

For Hosts

To list your property, create a host account, click on "List Your Property," and follow the step-by-step process to add your property details, photos, and pricing.

Hosts receive payment after the guest checks in. Payments are processed through our secure payment system and can be transferred to your bank account or mobile banking account.

To list a property, you need to provide valid identification, proof of ownership or permission to rent, and meet our basic property standards. All properties must have essential amenities and be in good condition.
