About Otithi

Your trusted platform for finding the perfect accommodation in Bangladesh

Our Story

Otithi was founded with a simple mission: to connect travelers with authentic Bangladeshi hospitality. We believe that the best way to experience Bangladesh is through its people and their warm hospitality.

Our platform brings together property owners and travelers, creating meaningful connections and unforgettable experiences.

We believe in authentic experiences that showcase the real Bangladesh.

Trust

Building trust through verified properties and secure transactions.

Community

Creating a community of hosts and guests who share our values.

Quality

Maintaining high standards in every aspect of our service.

Our Team

We are a team of passionate individuals dedicated to making your stay in Bangladesh memorable. From customer support to property verification, our team works tirelessly to ensure the best experience for both hosts and guests.
